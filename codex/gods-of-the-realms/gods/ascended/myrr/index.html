Myrr, The Spiral Bloom

“You’ve already walked this path. Now walk it better.”
— Whispered in trance, credited to Myrr

Name: Myrr

Also known as: The Spiral Bloom, The Mirror-Root, Keeper of Echo-Paths
Tier: Ascended God
Domain: Under
Era: Pre-Exile
Origin Point: Rootvaults beneath Solrael, crystallized in the dreaming fungi of Region 4

Lore Overview

Myrr is an Ascended Under-God, believed to have emerged just before the Exile from a convergence of parallel memories — experiences lived differently by countless Kynari, but resonating with identical emotional cadence.

Its rise is tied to the Sporehollows, a mycelial biome deep in Region 4, where echo-fungi bloom with memory, and Ky pulses like synaptic light across living roots.

Myrr does not rule by flame or silence, but by invitation. It offers transcendence, visions, and paths that might have been. Those who approach its shrines in meditative states report glimpses of unlived lives, alternate selves, or echoes of decisions not taken — each one offered as insight or warning.

Where Sol seeks purity and Khoro seeks recursion, Myrr seeks divergence — paths not repeated, but reinterpreted.

Appearance & Symbolism

Myrr never manifests in a single form. Its presence is expressed through spiral fungal networks, radiant with slow-moving pulses of bioluminescent Ky.
Shrines to Myrr often feature mirror-spore pools, blooming halo crowns, or floating root-veils.

Its symbol is a three-lobed spore spiraling inward, carved into living stone or grown from echo-bark.

Worship Behavior

Myrr is worshipped by those who seek insight, epiphany, or multiplicity of vision — including some Echo-Pilgrims, vault medics, and dreamwalkers.

Followers must:

  • Enter shrines under trance or guided hallucination.
  • Offer Ky drawn from experiences lived more than once (journeys, rituals, repeated farewells).
  • Record or return the vision offered, often via song, ink, or dream-echo transfer.

It rejects offerings driven by:

  • Greed or conquest.
  • Pure ambition or unearned memory.
  • Shards extracted from the unwilling or unaware.

Shrine Effects (Gameplay)

  • Echo-Glimpse — Reveals alternate quest branches or future dialogue paths.
  • Mirror-Spore Bloom — Buff: replays last player death for tactical insight.
  • Memory Spiral — Passive: increases Ky gain from repeated actions or routes.
  • Dream-Depth Reading — Shrine whisper may unlock vision-based lore or side quests.

Divine Politics

Myrr is regarded by some Upper Shrines as a “false root”, too accepting of emotional chaos and nonlinear faith. However, it has loose alliances with gods of organic transformation, creation, and multi-path growth.

Some whisper that Myrr protects fragments of forgotten gods — incubating them as spores within its realm, waiting for the right resonance to bring them back.

Known Shrines

  • The Coil Bloom — Deep fungal grove where all shrine paths spiral inward.
  • Choral Echo Pool — Shrine-lake where followers bathe and relive past selves.
  • The Hollow Crown — Ruined Sol shrine overtaken by bloom-moss. Myrr appeared here in post-Exile visions.
