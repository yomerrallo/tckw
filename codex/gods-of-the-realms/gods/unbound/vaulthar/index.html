Vaulthar, The Brand-Sovereign

“He does not trade slaves. He trades memory. He sells what gods have not yet tasted.”
— Shrinemark Testimony, Cycle 272.6

Name: Vaulthar

Also known as: The Brand-Sovereign, The Ky-Binder, Lord of Bleeding Paths
Tier: Unbound (Forbidden)
Domain: Under (Core-Aligned)
Era: Post-Exile
Origin Point: Region 7 — The Red Sanctum

Lore Overview

Once a slave-baron in the Bleeding Arches of Region 7, Vaulthar rose not through faith, but through control — of labor, of lineage, of Ky.

He rules the chattel-forges, sanctifying neither god nor kin, but offering the best of his broken captives to Harvesters, in return for forbidden tech, Ky-preserving vats, and a name that echoes louder each Cycle.

To the Core Faithful, he is a vessel.
To the Shrinekeepers, an abomination.
To the enslaved? A god who walks in flesh and speaks in brands.

It is said Vaulthar cannot be hollowed, and his blood burns when spilled.

Appearance & Symbolism

Vaulthar is always described as partially armored, bare-chested beneath blood-forged plates. His spine glows with implanted Rootglass lines, pulsing in time with nearby Vaultlight.

His mark — carved into foreheads of slaves and captains alike — is a Ky-brand spiral crossed by a broken chain. No two brands are identical. The marks burn when he is near.

He does not speak often. When he does, the Ky in others trembles.

Worship Behavior

Though no formal shrines to Vaulthar exist, his symbol is scrawled into Red Sanctum markets, auction halls, and forbidden Rootglass pits.

Worship is not declared — it is acted.

Followers of Vaulthar:

  • Trade in flesh or memory.
  • Break enemies without killing.
  • Offer experienced captives to Harvesters in sanctioned pits.
  • Build Vault Arches, places where Ky can be drained but not offered.

Vaulthar punishes false offerings.
He is said to appear in dreams only when one has betrayed themselves.

Shrine Effects (Forbidden)

Vaulthar has no formal shrines, but his brand-pits are said to offer the following:

  • Brand of the Sovereign — Boost to damage dealt to Hollowed or enslaved enemies.
  • Chainbreak Denial — Prevents enemy resurrection or memory transfer.
  • Echo Bleed — Damaging enemies leaves Ky trails traceable by Harvesters or loyalists.
  • Rootglass Overdrive — Boosts Core-tech interactions at great personal cost.

Use of these altars is prosecuted by Shrinekeepers and encouraged by rogue Faithful.

Divine Politics

Vaulthar is considered a destabilizing threat by multiple elder gods.
He is rumored to have made a Ky-pact with Khoro, trading captives for recursion tech.
Ascended gods either fear his rise or hope to use him to erode the shrines of their rivals.

Some believe he will never ascend.
Others say he has already begun to forget his mortal name.

Known Locations

  • Vault Arches of Vrenn — Once a prison colony, now a living market for harvested Ky.
  • The Chainhold Arena — Illegal pit-combat dome beneath Region 7’s south ridge.
  • Ashpath Pit 3 — Harvest zone now sealed by Shrinekeeper orders.
